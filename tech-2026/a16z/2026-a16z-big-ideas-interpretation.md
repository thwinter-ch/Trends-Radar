## Essence of the Report

Andreessen Horowitz's Big Ideas 2026 is a three-part series featuring 17 partners sharing predictions across AI, enterprise, physical world, crypto, and security. Unlike consultancy reports, this is explicitly a VC perspective — smart money showing where it wants to invest.

The format is distinctive: individual partners stake reputations on specific predictions. This creates accountability rare in trend reports. The ideas are genuinely novel in several cases, reflecting deep technical expertise in the firm.

The "interactive AI video" prediction stands out — video that understands time, remembers context, reacts to input. Characters persist, physics hold, consequences unfold. This is a meaningful evolution beyond today's generative video.

"System of record loses primacy" is a bold enterprise call: AI collapsing distance between intent and execution, reading/writing/reasoning directly across operational data. ITSM and CRM becoming autonomous workflow engines. If true, this disrupts massive incumbent software categories.

## Key Findings

### AI & Infrastructure
- **Interactive AI Video** — Video stops being passive. Models understand time, remember context, react to input. Characters persist, physics hold.
- **Agent-Native Infrastructure** — Shift from human-speed traffic to "agent-speed" workloads. Recursive, bursty, massive. Biggest infrastructure shock comes from within.
- **Multi-Agent Systems** — Enterprises shift from isolated AI tools to coordinated digital teams.

### Enterprise Disruption
- **System of Record Loses Primacy** — AI collapses distance between intent and execution. Models read, write, reason directly across operational data.
- **AI-Native University** — First institution built from ground up around intelligent systems expected in 2026.

### Physical World
- **American Manufacturing Renaissance** — Rebirth of the factory with software and AI at its heart. Modular deployment alongside skilled workers.

### Crypto & Finance
- **Origination, Not Just Tokenization** — Stablecoins mainstream in 2025; 2026 sees native crypto financial instruments.
- **zkVM Provers Hit 10,000X Overhead** — Fast enough for phones, cheap enough for everywhere. Real-time proofs of CPU execution.

### Security
- **AI Closes the Cybersecurity Hiring Gap** — Automating repetitive work frees security teams for actual threat hunting.
