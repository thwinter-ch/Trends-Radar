## Quick Summary

- Only 12% of CEOs report both cost AND revenue benefits from AI
- 56% report no significant financial benefit from AI
- Companies achieving dual benefits are 2-3x more likely to have embedded AI extensively
- CEO confidence at five-year low (30% confident in revenue growth)

**Bias Level:** Medium-High (Big Four consultancy)
