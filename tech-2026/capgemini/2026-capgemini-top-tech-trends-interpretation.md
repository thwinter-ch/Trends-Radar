# Capgemini: Top Tech Trends 2026 — Interpretation

## Overview

Capgemini's tech trends report positions AI as the "backbone" of enterprise digital infrastructure, with Cloud 3.0 and intelligent applications as key themes.

## Key Themes

### AI Backbone
AI as foundational infrastructure rather than point solution. Aligns with broader industry shift from "AI projects" to "AI-native operations."

### Intelligent Applications
Evolution of applications with embedded AI capabilities. Standard enterprise software trajectory.

### Cloud 3.0
Next evolution of cloud computing — likely hybrid/multi-cloud, edge integration, and AI-optimized infrastructure.

### Dual-Use Defense Technology
Commercial applications of defense tech and vice versa. Interesting geopolitical angle.

### Technology Sovereignty
Data localization, regional AI capabilities, reduced dependence on foreign tech. Aligns with Gartner's "geopatriation" trend.
