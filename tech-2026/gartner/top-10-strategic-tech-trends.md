**Date Reviewed:** January 21, 2026

## The 10 Trends (Three Themes)

### The Architect (Building Foundations)

**1. AI-Native Development Platforms**
Embedding GenAI directly into development. Engineers collaborate with AI to write, test, optimize code.
- *Prediction:* By 2030, 80% of orgs will transform large engineering teams into smaller, AI-enhanced teams.

**2. AI Supercomputing Platforms**
Integrating AI-powered CPUs, GPUs, ASICs, neuromorphic computing.
- *Prediction:* By 2028, 40%+ of leading enterprises will adopt hybrid computing paradigm architectures.

**3. Confidential Computing**
Hardware-based trusted execution environments (TEEs) for sensitive data.
- *Prediction:* By 2029, 75%+ of operations on untrusted infrastructure protected by confidential computing.

### The Synthesist (Orchestrating Intelligent Systems)

**4. Multiagent Systems**
Modular AI agents collaborating on complex tasks.
- *What they're selling:* Research subscriptions, advisory on agent architecture.

**5. Domain-Specific Language Models**
Higher accuracy and compliance for industry-specific use cases.
- *Prediction:* By 2028, 50%+ of enterprise GenAI models will be domain-specific.

**6. Physical AI**
Intelligence in the real world — robots, drones, smart equipment.

### The Sentinel (Protecting Value)

**7. Preemptive Cybersecurity**
AI-powered proactive threat blocking.
- *Prediction:* By 2030, preemptive solutions = 50% of all security spending.

**8. Digital Provenance**
Verifying origin, ownership, integrity of software/data/media.

**9. AI Security Platforms**
Centralized visibility, usage policies, protection against AI-specific risks.
- *Prediction:* By 2028, 50%+ of companies will use AI security platforms.

**10. Geopatriation**
Moving workloads from global clouds to local/sovereign infrastructure.
- *Prediction:* By 2030, 75%+ of enterprises in Europe/Middle East will geopatriate workloads.

## Commercial Bias Analysis

### Why This Report Exists

Gartner's business model: sell research subscriptions and advisory services. The Top 10 list:
1. Creates FOMO — "you need to track these trends"
2. Drives inquiries to Gartner analysts
3. Establishes vocabulary that vendors then adopt

### The Prediction Game

Every trend comes with a prediction (always 2028-2030). These create urgency while being far enough out to avoid accountability.

### What's Genuinely Useful

- **The three-theme framework** (Architect/Synthesist/Sentinel) is a good mental model
- **Geopatriation** is an underrated trend others miss
- **Confidential computing** is technically important and often overlooked
- **Domain-specific LLMs** is a contrarian call worth watching
