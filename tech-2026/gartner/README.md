## Quick Summary

10 trends in 3 themes:
- **The Architect:** AI-native dev, AI supercomputing, confidential computing
- **The Synthesist:** Multiagent systems, domain-specific LLMs, physical AI
- **The Sentinel:** Preemptive security, digital provenance, AI security platforms, geopatriation
