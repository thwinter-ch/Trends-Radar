# IBM: Business and Technology Trends 2026

**Report:** Business and Technology Trends 2026
**Publisher:** IBM Institute for Business Value
**URL:** https://www.ibm.com/thought-leadership/institute-business-value/en-us/report/business-trends-2026
**Date Reviewed:** January 21, 2026

---

## Survey Scope

- 1,000+ C-suite executives
- 8,500 global consumers and employees

---

## Key Findings

### Quantum Computing

**"Quantum advantage is likely to emerge by the end of 2026"**

The point at which a quantum computer provides demonstrable improvement over classical methods.

### AI Sovereignty

**"93% of executives say they must factor AI sovereignty into their 2026 business strategy"**

Reflecting concerns about:
- Data residency
- Regulatory compliance
- Geopolitical risk
- Supply chain control

---

## Commercial Bias Analysis

### Why This Report Exists

IBM is:
1. A major enterprise technology vendor
2. Investing heavily in quantum computing
3. Positioning watsonx as enterprise AI platform

The report:
1. Positions IBM as thought leader in enterprise tech
2. Creates urgency for quantum (where IBM leads)
3. Validates "sovereignty" concerns that favor on-prem/hybrid (IBM's sweet spot)

### The Quantum Push

IBM has significant quantum investments. The "quantum advantage by end of 2026" prediction serves their roadmap.

### The Sovereignty Angle

"AI sovereignty" aligns perfectly with IBM's hybrid cloud, on-premises positioning against hyperscalers.

### What's Genuinely Useful

- **Large consumer sample** (8,500) adds demand-side perspective
- **Quantum timeline** is specific and falsifiable
- **Sovereignty framing** captures real enterprise concerns
- **C-suite + employee views** provides organizational depth

---

## What's Missing
