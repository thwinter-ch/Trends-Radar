## Six Trends Rewriting Banking's Future

### 1. Smart Money Revolution
- Digital currencies (stablecoins, CBDCs, tokenized deposits) reshape money movement
- Banks shift from adoption to building connected, intelligent transfer systems

- **57% of banking IT executives** expect broad AI agent adoption in risk, compliance, fraud detection within 3 years

### 3. GPT-Powered Interfaces
- **65% of customers** open to GPT-like financial assistants
- Banks must modernize cores to deliver real-time, context-aware interactions

### 4. Unconstrained Banking
- Digital assets and AI enable new business models
- Transcend traditional banking boundaries

### 5. Risk Interconnectivity
- **81% of risk executives** expect more interconnected risks
- Only **38% satisfied** with business adoption of risk mindset

### 6. Balance Sheet Battle
- Fintechs, stablecoins, and private credit target core banking products
- Banks must rethink balance sheet management and forge new partnerships
