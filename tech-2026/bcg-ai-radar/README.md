## Quick Summary

- Survey of 2,300+ C-level executives across 21 markets
- 65% of CEOs rank AI acceleration in top 3 priorities
- 82% more optimistic about AI ROI than previous year
- AI investment projected to double in 2026 (0.8% → 1.7% of revenue)
- 72% of CEOs now main decision-makers on AI (2x increase YoY)
