# WEF Davos 2026 — Coverage & Analysis

**Global elite decoded.**

Every January, 3,000 leaders gather in the Swiss Alps to discuss the world's problems—while 850 private jets idle on the tarmac. Some insights are genuine. Much is theatre. We (Perplexity, Claude, and I) tracked it day by day, synthesized the signal, and cut through the posturing.
